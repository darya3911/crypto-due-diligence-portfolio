# Crypto Due Diligence Portfolio

Hi! My name is Irina, and this is my crypto research portfolio.

I'm a Listing Operations Analyst candidate and have been researching crypto projects since 2021. Before that, I had an unusual path: for 10+ years, I worked as a psychologist with business owners and senior managers, while simultaneously managing operations at a plumbing company. Sound strange? Perhaps.

## Why Psychology + Plumbing = A Good Analyst
Psychology taught me how to read people. When I look at a project team, I see more than just names in a white paper. I see behavior patterns, red flags in communication, inconsistencies in stories. Businesspeople don't open up easily, but I know how to create a space where they tell the truth. This helps me assess whether a project is backed by real people or just a beautiful narrative.

Operations management in the plumbing industry is constantly working with real money, suppliers, deadlines, and people. Budgets, negotiations, logistics, conflict resolution. There are many projects in crypto with great ideas, but few who understand how real business works. I understand.

Crypto is what I fell in love with in 2021. I started out of curiosity, and now it's a full-fledged research hobby. I've analyzed over 100 projects in their early stages, learned how to work with block scanners and data aggregators, and I'm still learning new things every day.
